PASADO TO BE vs PASADO SIMPLE

Uso del WAS - WERE - DID


***Pasado To BE - Ser o Estar***

I* - HE - SHE - IT --> WAS
YOU - WE - THEY --> WERE

En algunos casos al usar CONDICIONALES el
Were tambien puede usarse con el I y también 
en el uso de Wish


***Pasado Simple***

I - YOU - HE - SHE - IT - WE - THEY --> DID

No mezclar con el verbo To Be --> am - are
Trabaja con Verbos Regulares e Irregulares

-----------Ejemplos-------------
1. Yo no (estaba) en mi casa.
- I was not in my house.

2. Nosotros -estudiamos- ayer.
+ We studied yesterday

3. ¿(Estabas) en el supermercado?
? Were you at/in the supermarket?

4. ¿Compraste la carne?
? Did you buy the meat?

5. Andres no estaba con nosotros. 
- He wasn't with us.       us: Pronombre objeto

6. Erika no entendía este tema.
- She didn't understand this topic.

7. ¿Estaban ellos visitando a su padre?
? Were they visiting their father?

8. ¿Manejaste solo?
? Did you driver alone?

9. ¿Dónde estabas anoche?
? Where were you last nigth?
