EL PASADO SIMPLE / SIMPLE PAST

(Acciones o actividades que ya ocurrieron)

Se usa el AUXILIAR --- DID 

1. En oraciones:
Negativas e Interrogativas y en algunas respuestas

2. En Verbos regulares:
Estos terminan en ED y cumplen una regla

3. En Verbos Irregulares:
Aprender los que mas uses


***Ejemplo con Verbo Regular***

.El cerró la puerta
+ He closED the door.
- He did not (didn't) close the door.
? Did he close the door? -> El cerró la puerta?
? Didn't he close the door? -> El no cerró la puerta?
Yes, he closed the door.
No, he didn't close the door.


***Ejemplo con Verbo Irregular***

.Ellos comieron pizza.
+ They ate pizza.
- They didn't eat pizza. //Al llevar did se coloca el verbo en su forma normal.
? Did they eat pizza?
Yes, they did.
No, they didn't.
